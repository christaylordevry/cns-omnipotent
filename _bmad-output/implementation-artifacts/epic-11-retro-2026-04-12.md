## What the epic delivered

Phase 2.0 **Epic D:** three **read-only Bases** panels under `_meta/bases/` (inbox triage, project status with `groupBy: file.folder`, research tracker with nested `or` on `pake_type`) plus a **`_README.md`** that states purpose, panel list, and why inline Bases editing is **not** endorsed as a governed write path. Vault artifact only; no Vault IO or `src/` changes. Operator smoke on **2026-04-05** was **full green** (Obsidian **1.9.10**; row counts and AC behavior recorded in the story file).

## Systems lessons

- **`_meta/` is the right home** for operator infrastructure views: contract-aligned, excluded from NotebookLM bulk export (pairs cleanly with Epic 10’s exporter), and naturally **agent-structural-deny** under WriteGate so “completion” stays **human-apply + verify**, not silent MCP mkdir.
- **Bases is still “product UI” with semver drift:** YAML that parses in `js-yaml` can still differ by Obsidian build. Capturing **app version + smoke outcomes** in the story record made this epic auditable without automated E2E.
- **Read ≠ safe to edit:** Bases can mutate frontmatter on disk and **bypass** WriteGate, PAKE validation, and mutation audit. Calling that out in `_README.md` is governance, not paranoia; it sets expectations until a future design explicitly bridges Bases and Vault IO.

## Continuity from Epic 10

Epic 10 optimized **research ingress and export**. Epic 11 gives **at-a-glance triage** over the same PAKE-shaped notes (`InsightNote` / `SourceNote` literals aligned with `src/pake/schemas.ts`). Together they reduce “open five folders” friction without pretending Obsidian reads are MCP.

## What to carry forward

1. For any **Obsidian-native** artifact story, bundle **canonical YAML in the story record**, **copy paths**, and **operator smoke bullets** the way 11-1 did; it scales better than chat-only delivery.  
2. When adding panels later, update `_meta/bases/_README.md` in the same change set so new operators see one index.  
3. Re-run smoke after **Obsidian minor upgrades** if Bases syntax changes; adjust filters in-repo and re-record version in the Dev Agent Record.

## Challenges (resolved in delivery)

- **Filter expressiveness:** Nested `or` for `pake_type` could have forced a two-file fallback; the shipped YAML worked on first try. Keep the fallback plan in the story template for the next panel that pushes filter limits.  
- **Inbox rows without `created`:** Daily-style captures show **blank** `created`; that is correct per AC (include row, do not hide). Document for operators so they do not mistake it for a broken panel.
