## Systems lessons

- **Charter-before-code is the correct Phase 2.1 wedge:** embeddings touch **WriteGate spirit** even when they are reads at the filesystem layer; stating **default `realpath` / host-boundary** posture early avoids “helpful” indexers that ignore protected path classes.  
- **Index placement outside the vault** as default reduces fork risk and keeps the vault contract from silently absorbing gigabyte-scale artifacts.  
- **Review-driven deferrals are assets:** Nexus-origin detection, concurrent mutation during index build, and embedding model version in manifests were **deferred with reasons**, giving the SM backlog honest titles instead of hiding unknowns inside “future work” prose.
